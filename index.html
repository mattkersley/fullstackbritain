Code of Conduct

TL;DR

Be kind. Be helpful. Be respectful. Be careful.

Anything considered hurtful, abusive or disrespectful will not be tollerated and action will be taken by a community admin.

Be Kind.

Our aim is to provide a polite and considerate community.
This means we need everyone to treat others as they wish to be treated (unless you're into being treated badly, you filthy animal).
Basically, this means don't be abusive in any fashion.

Be Respectful.

Admins will not tollerate disrespectful behaviour.

  • No abusive language
  • No sexist comments
  • No racism
  • No targetted ill-will (bullying)

If someone is bothering you, ask politely for them to stop. If they continue, notify an admin and it will be dealt with impartially, please respect the admin's decision.

Be Careful.

Don't give out any personal or financial information to strangers. Use your common sense.

It's easy to become "familiar" with people through online conversation, but remember, you may not know the person you're talking to.

If you want to talk business with someone and trade services, do so in a private channel or DM.
